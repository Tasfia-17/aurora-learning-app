## How It Works: From Notes to Magic

The core of Aurora is simple yet powerful:

1.  **Bring Your Knowledge:** Head to the Creation Hub and paste, type, or upload a file containing your notes.
2.  **Let Aurora Conjure:** Choose from a wide array of activities.
3.  **Play & Learn:** Aurora's powerful AI (powered by the Google Gemini API) instantly analyzes your text and builds a custom game or challenge based on your material.
